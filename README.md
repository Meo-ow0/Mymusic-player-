🎀 Welcome to My Music Station!

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by. This is my little digital workspace a project I built to keep my music, my notes, and my aesthetic all in one place. It’s basically my "cozy corner" on the web.
୨୧ What's inside?
I designed this to be simple and sweet:

The Music Station: A place to keep my favorite tracks organized.

The Writing Corner: Somewhere to dump my thoughts during the day. It saves what I write automatically, so I never lose my ideas!

Personalization: I love to change things up, so I added a way to upload my own art to the banner.

Live Clock: Just a little reminder of the time while I'm focused on my work.
⟡ To built
I made this using standard HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It was a fun challenge to get everything feeling just right, especially the "desktop" window look!

☆ How to use this
It's super easy to get it running:

Just download the files to your computer.

Open index.html in any web browser.

That's it! Everything should load right up and be ready for you to use.

💌 A little note
I hope you find this as relaxing to use as I did to build. If you have any suggestions or just want to see how I’m building it, feel free to look around the code!
